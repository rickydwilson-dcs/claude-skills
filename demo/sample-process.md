# Customer Onboarding Process

**Process Owner:** Customer Success Manager
**Description:** End-to-end process for onboarding new enterprise customers from contract signing through successful activation and first value delivery.

## Steps

1. Receive signed contract and customer details (Sales Team) - 30 minutes
   Input: Signed contract, customer contact information
   Output: Customer record in CRM

2. Create customer account in all systems (Operations Analyst) - 45 minutes
   Input: Customer details from CRM
   Output: Account IDs for all platforms

3. Schedule kickoff meeting with customer stakeholders (Customer Success Manager) - 2 hours
   Input: Customer contact list
   Output: Calendar invite, meeting agenda

4. Conduct kickoff meeting and gather requirements (Customer Success Manager) - 1 hour
   Input: Meeting agenda, discovery questionnaire
   Output: Requirements document, success metrics

5. If custom integration required? Configure integration settings (Solutions Engineer) - 4 hours
   Input: Integration requirements
   Output: Integration configuration, API credentials

6. Create training plan based on customer needs (Training Specialist) - 2 hours
   Input: Requirements document
   Output: Training schedule, materials list

7. Deliver initial training sessions (Training Specialist) - 8 hours
   Input: Training materials
   Output: Trained users, completion certificates

8. Validate customer can achieve first use case (Customer Success Manager) - 2 hours
   Input: Success metrics from kickoff
   Output: Validation report, go-live approval

9. Hand off to ongoing support team (Customer Success Manager) - 30 minutes
   Input: Customer documentation
   Output: Support ticket created, intro email sent

## Roles
- Sales Team
- Operations Analyst
- Customer Success Manager
- Solutions Engineer
- Training Specialist
