## Quick Reference: Engagement Strategies by Quadrant

### High Power / High Interest (Manage Closely)
- **Frequency:** Weekly or bi-weekly
- **Method:** Face-to-face meetings, decision workshops
- **Content:** Detailed updates, strategic discussions, co-create solutions
- **Goal:** Active partnership and advocacy

### High Power / Low Interest (Keep Satisfied)
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Method:** Executive summaries, brief check-ins
- **Content:** High-level status, impact on their priorities
- **Goal:** Maintain support, avoid surprises

### Low Power / High Interest (Keep Informed)
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Method:** Email updates, town halls, Q&A sessions
- **Content:** Detailed progress, opportunities to provide input
- **Goal:** Keep engaged and satisfied as advocates

### Low Power / Low Interest (Monitor)
- **Frequency:** Quarterly or as-needed
- **Method:** General communications, newsletters
- **Content:** Project existence, major milestones
- **Goal:** Awareness without over-communication
