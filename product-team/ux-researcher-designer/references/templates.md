# UX Research Templates

Templates for user research, personas, journey maps, and usability testing.

## User Interview Script Template

```markdown
# User Interview Script

## Introduction (5 min)
Hi [Name], thanks for joining! I'm [Your Name], and I'm researching [topic] to improve [product/feature]. There are no right or wrong answers - I'm just trying to understand your experience.

I'll record this for note-taking. Everything is confidential. Sound good?

## Background (5 min)
1. Tell me about your role - what are you responsible for?
2. Walk me through a typical day/week
3. What tools do you use regularly for [task area]?

## Problem Exploration (20 min)
4. What are the biggest challenges you face with [problem area]?
   ‚Üí Follow-up: Tell me about the last time that happened
   ‚Üí Follow-up: How often does this come up?

5. Walk me through your current process for [specific task]
   ‚Üí Probe: What's frustrating about that?
   ‚Üí Probe: Where do things usually go wrong?

6. Have you tried other solutions?
   ‚Üí Follow-up: What did you try?
   ‚Üí Follow-up: Why didn't that work?

## Solution Validation (10 min - Optional)
7. [Show concept] What's your initial reaction?
   ‚Üí Follow-up: How would this fit into your workflow?
   ‚Üí Follow-up: What concerns do you have?

8. On a scale of 1-10, how much would this improve your workflow?
   ‚Üí Follow-up: What would make it a 10?

## Wrap-Up (5 min)
9. Is there anything I didn't ask about that I should have?
10. Who else should I talk to about this problem?
11. Can I follow up with you as we develop this?
```

## Jobs-to-be-Done

[High/Medium/Low] - Based on [X] interviews

## Usability Test Plan Template

```markdown
# Usability Test Plan

## Research Questions
1. [Question about usability/findability]
2. [Question about comprehension]
3. [Question about satisfaction]

## Success Metrics
- Task completion rate: [Target %]
- Time on task: [Target time]
- Error rate: [Target %]
- SUS score: [Target score]

## Participants
**Number:** 5-8 per round
**Criteria:**
- [Screening criterion 1]
- [Screening criterion 2]

**Compensation:** [Amount/method]

## Test Scenarios

### Task 1: [Task Name]
**Scenario:** "[Realistic scenario description]"
**Success Criteria:** [What counts as successful completion]
**Time Limit:** [Optional]

### Task 2: [Task Name]
[Repeat structure]

## Test Environment
- **Platform:** [Desktop/Mobile/Tablet]
- **Browser:** [Browsers to test]
- **Prototype:** [Link to prototype]
- **Recording:** [Screen + audio]

## Session Structure
1. Introduction (5 min)
2. Background questions (5 min)
3. Task scenarios (25 min)
4. Post-task questions (10 min)
5. Wrap-up (5 min)

**Total Duration:** 50 minutes

## Post-Task Questions
1. How easy or difficult was that task? (1-7 scale)
2. What was confusing, if anything?
3. What would you improve?
```
