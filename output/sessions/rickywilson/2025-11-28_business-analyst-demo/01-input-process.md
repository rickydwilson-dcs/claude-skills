# Customer Onboarding Process

## Overview
Process for onboarding new enterprise customers from contract signature to go-live.

## Process Owner
Sarah Johnson, Customer Success Director

## Steps

### Step 1: Contract Received
- **Role**: Sales Operations
- **Duration**: 1 day
- **Input**: Signed contract from Sales
- **Output**: Customer record in CRM
- **Tools**: Salesforce CRM

### Step 2: Welcome Package
- **Role**: Customer Success Manager
- **Duration**: 2 days  
- **Input**: Customer record
- **Output**: Welcome email sent, kickoff scheduled
- **Tools**: HubSpot, Calendly

### Step 3: Technical Setup
- **Role**: Solutions Engineer
- **Duration**: 5 days
- **Input**: Technical requirements document
- **Output**: Environment configured
- **Tools**: AWS Console, Terraform

### Step 4: Data Migration
- **Role**: Data Engineer
- **Duration**: 7 days
- **Input**: Source data files
- **Output**: Data migrated and validated
- **Tools**: Python, SQL

### Step 5: Training
- **Role**: Training Specialist
- **Duration**: 3 days
- **Input**: Training curriculum
- **Output**: Users trained, certification complete
- **Tools**: LMS, Zoom

### Step 6: Go-Live
- **Role**: Customer Success Manager
- **Duration**: 1 day
- **Input**: All checklist items complete
- **Output**: Customer using production system
- **Tools**: Jira, Confluence

## Stakeholders
- VP of Customer Success (Sponsor)
- IT Director (Approver)
- End Users (Informed)
- Finance Team (Consulted)

## KPIs
- Time to Value: Target 21 days
- Customer Satisfaction: Target 4.5/5
- First Week Engagement: Target 80%
