# CEO Executive Templates

Ready-to-use templates for CEO communications, reporting, and planning.

## Daily CEO Schedule Template

```
6:00 AM - Personal Development
- Reading (industry news, books)
- Exercise (30-45 minutes)
- Meditation/reflection

7:00 AM - Day Planning & Priority Review
- Review calendar
- Set top 3 priorities
- Check critical metrics

8:00 AM - Metric Dashboard Review
- Financial KPIs
- Customer metrics
- Team health indicators
- Risk flags

8:30 AM - Customer/Market Intelligence
- Customer feedback review
- Competitive intelligence
- Market trends
- Sales pipeline

9:00 AM - Strategic Work Block
- Deep thinking time
- Strategy development
- Important non-urgent work
- NO MEETINGS

10:30 AM - Meetings Block
- Executive team sync
- 1-on-1s with directs
- Cross-functional alignment
- Decision-making sessions

12:00 PM - Lunch (Networking/Thinking)
- Customer/partner lunches
- Team bonding
- Or protected thinking time

1:00 PM - External Meetings
- Investor relations
- Board member check-ins
- Customer meetings
- Partner discussions

3:00 PM - Internal Meetings
- Department reviews
- Project check-ins
- Problem solving
- Team support

4:30 PM - Email/Communication
- Email responses
- Slack/messaging
- Review documents
- Approve decisions

5:30 PM - Team Walk-Around
- Informal check-ins
- Visible leadership
- Team pulse check
- Relationship building

6:00 PM - Transition/Reflection
- Day review
- Tomorrow preparation
- Personal time begins
```

## All-Hands Meeting Template

### Structure (45 minutes)

**Opening (5 min)**
```markdown
- Welcome and agenda
- Celebration of wins
- Recognition of team members
- Set positive tone
```

**Metrics Review (10 min)**
```markdown
- Financial performance
- Customer growth
- Product metrics

- Context and trends
```

**Strategic Updates (15 min)**
```markdown
- Company strategy refresh
- Major initiatives progress
- Market updates
- Competitive landscape
- Strategic decisions/changes
```

**Department Spotlights (10 min)**
```markdown
- Rotating department presentations
- Recent wins and learnings
- Cross-functional appreciation
- Upcoming focus areas
```

**Q&A (15 min)**
```markdown
- Open questions from team
- Anonymous questions welcome
- Transparent answers

```

**Closing (5 min)**
```markdown
- Key takeaways (3 points)
- Next all-hands date
- Social/team event reminder
- Thank you and inspiration
```

## Board Meeting Preparation Checklist

### T-4 Weeks (Agenda Development)
- [ ] Draft initial agenda with board chair
- [ ] Identify key decision items
- [ ] List strategic discussion topics
- [ ] Confirm meeting logistics
- [ ] Schedule prep meetings with CFO/executives

### T-2 Weeks (Material Preparation)
- [ ] Finalize board package outline
- [ ] Request inputs from leadership team
- [ ] Draft CEO letter
- [ ] Compile financial reports
- [ ] Update strategic initiative slides
- [ ] Prepare risk register
- [ ] Draft discussion materials for key topics

### T-1 Week (Package Distribution)
- [ ] Finalize all materials
- [ ] Get CFO sign-off on financials
- [ ] Proofread entire package
- [ ] Upload to board portal
- [ ] Send notification to board members
- [ ] Schedule pre-meeting calls with board members
- [ ] Prepare talking points for each section

### T-0 (Meeting Execution)
- [ ] Test technology setup
- [ ] Confirm attendee list
- [ ] Print backup materials
- [ ] Brief presenters
- [ ] Execute agenda
- [ ] Capture action items
- [ ] Thank board members

### T+2 Days (Follow-up)
- [ ] Distribute meeting minutes
- [ ] Send action item tracker
- [ ] Schedule follow-ups as needed
- [ ] File materials in board portal
- [ ] Debrief with team on decisions

## Fundraising Pitch Deck Template

### Slide-by-Slide Guide

**Slide 1: Cover**
- Company name and tagline
- CEO name and contact
- Date and confidential notice

**Slide 2: Problem (The Pain)**
- Compelling problem statement
- Market evidence of pain
- Current inadequate solutions
- Cost of the problem

**Slide 3: Solution (Your Product)**
- Your unique solution
- How it solves the problem
- Key differentiators
- Demo or screenshot

**Slide 4-5: Market Opportunity**
- Total addressable market (TAM)
- Serviceable addressable market (SAM)
- Serviceable obtainable market (SOM)
- Market growth trends
- Why now?

**Slide 6-7: Product (Deep Dive)**
- Product features and benefits
- Technology/IP
- Product roadmap
- Customer testimonials

**Slide 8: Business Model**
- Revenue model
- Pricing strategy
- Unit economics
- Sales process

**Slide 9-10: Go-to-Market Strategy**
- Customer acquisition channels
- Sales and marketing strategy
- Growth strategy
- Expansion plans

**Slide 11: Competition**
- Competitive landscape
- Your positioning
- Competitive advantages
- Barriers to entry

**Slide 12: Team**
- Founders and key executives
- Relevant experience
- Advisory board
- Key hires planned

**Slide 13-14: Financials**
- Historical performance
- Current metrics
- 3-year projections
- Key assumptions

**Slide 15: The Ask**
- Amount raising
- Use of funds breakdown
- Milestones to achieve
- Expected timeline

**Appendix: Additional Slides**
- Detailed financial model
- Customer case studies
- Technology architecture
- Market research data
- Team bios
- Cap table
